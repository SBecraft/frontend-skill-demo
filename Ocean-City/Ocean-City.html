Ocean City Quick Facts:

  • Ocean City is known as the “White Marlin Capital of the World,” hosting the annual White Marlin Open—one of the world's largest fishing tournaments, where prize money for top catches can exceed $1 million.
  • In summer, Ocean City's population surges to over 300,000 visitors, with close to eight million people traveling there annually.
  • Before Ocean City was given its name in 1874, it was known as "The Lady's Resort to the Ocean."
  • In 1900 the Noxezema skincare product, then known as Prescription 22, was invented in Ocean City in 1900 by a resident pharmacist, Dr. Francis Townsend, to soothe sunburns. invented

Ocean City at a Glance


If you're looking for the perfect beach getaway, Ocean City, Maryland is honestly one of the coolest spots on the East Coast. It's right on a long barrier island along the Atlantic Ocean, so you get miles of sandy beaches, a super fun boardwalk, and tons of things to do whether you're into maritime attractions, boating, fishing, water sports, food, shopping, or just chilling by the water. What's really cool is that Ocean City is basically surrounded by water. You've got the Atlantic Ocean on one side and the calm, scenic Assawoman Bay on the other, which connects into the Chesapeake Bay system. It gives the whole area this awesome mix of ocean vibes and bay views all in one place.

One of the best parts about Ocean City is the weather. In the summer it's warm, sunny, and perfect for swimming or hanging out on the beach. Spring and fall are a little cooler but still really nice if you like fewer crowds. Even in the winter, it's a pretty awesome place to visit if you want peaceful ocean views without the summer rush.

Getting here is actually pretty easy. If you're coming from Maryland, Delaware, or Virginia, you can just drive straight in. Most people take Route 50, which brings you right over the bridge and onto the island. If you're farther away, the closest major airports are in Baltimore, Salisbury, and Philadelphia, and from there it's just a road trip away.

Basically, Ocean City is the kind of place where you can relax, explore, and make memories without trying too hard. It's got that classic beach-town vibe, and once you visit, you'll get why so many people come back every year.
